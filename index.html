Hi, welcome to my page! Here is a little bit about me!

My name is Abby Lewey, I'm from a small town known as Cameron, Missouri. I've grown up in Cameron all of my life. I was raised by my parents, Susan and Jeff, along with my 2 triplet sisters Becky and Cassy. I have 5 horses, 2 cats, and a dog. In my free time I love to read, sleep, exercise, take photos, and ride horses. As a student at Northwest since 2018, I have learned many things in three years i've been on campus. I started off as a Computer Science major my freshman year and then decided it wasnt something that I completely wanted to do with the rest of my life. For the past couple of years I introduced myself to photography after I was gifted a canon camera for my birthday. I took the camera everywhere I went and it became something I truly enjoyed. As my second semester of my freshman year rolled around I switched my major to IDM, New media- Digital media. From here, I've been learning so much things that I have grown to love. From this point on, I use my camera and take photos of family members that request pictures, friends, and even strangers. I started off doing landscape photography, portrait photography, and now events such as weddings, and family photo. My years at northwest in this field are rapidly coming to an end and I will forever cherish what ive succeeded in, as well as all the friends that have helped me along the way..

Here are a few things that I like to do.

  • Ride horses
  • Listen to music
  • Read (preferably mystery or romance)
  • Photography
